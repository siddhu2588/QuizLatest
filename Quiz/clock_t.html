CORRECT ANSWERS & ANALYSIS REPORT

  1. (b)22
    Explanation: The hands of a clock coincide only once between 11 O' clock and 1 O' clock, so in every 12 hours, the hands of a clock will coincide for 11 times.
    ∴ In a day or 24 hours, the hands of a clock will coincide for 22 (11+11) times.

    Angle traced by the hour hand in 3 hours 30 minutes = ((360/12)*3)+ (30/60)hours
    (360/12)*(21/6)=105°
    Angle traced by minute hand in 60 minutes = 360°
    Angel traced by minute hand in 30 minutes = (360/60)*30=180°
    ∴ Required angle would be = (180 - 105)° = 75°

  3. (c)10pm

    23 hrs 44 minutes = 356/15 hours
    Now, 356/15 Hrs of this clock = 24 hours of correct clock
    89 hours of this clock = ((24*15)/356) * 89 = 90 hours of the correct clock, i.e. the correct clock gains one hour over the incorrect clock.
    ∴The correct time on the fourth day would be 10pm.
