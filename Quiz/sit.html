CORRECT ANSWERS & ANALYSIS REPORT

  1. (c) Rs. 698
    Explanation: S.I. for 1 year = Rs. (854 - 815) = Rs. 39. S.I. for 3 years = Rs.(39 x 3) = Rs. 117. Principal = Rs. (815 - 117) = Rs. 698.

  2. (a) Can not be determined
    Explanation: We need to know the S.I., principal and time to find the rate. Since the principal is not given, so data is inadequate.

  3. (d) 12%
    Explanation: S.I. for 3 years = Rs. (12005 - 9800) = Rs. 2205. S.I. for 5 years = Rs. (2205/3)x 5 = Rs. 3675 Principal = Rs. (9800 - 3675) = Rs. 6125. Hence, rate = (100 x 3675)/(6125 X 5) % = 12%

  4. (a) 15000
    Explanation: Principal = Rs. (100 x 5400)/(12 X 3)= Rs. 15000.

  5. (d) 10.25%
    Explanation: Let the sum be Rs. 100. Then, S.I. for first 6 months = Rs. (100 x 10 x 1)/(100 X 2) = Rs. 5 S.I. for last 6 months = Rs. (105 x 10 x 1)/(100 X 2) = Rs. 5.25 So, amount at the end of 1 year = Rs. (100 + 5 + 5.25) = Rs. 110.25 Effective rate = (110.25 - 100) = 10.25%
